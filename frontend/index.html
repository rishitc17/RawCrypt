01 · THE PREMISE

A war of wits, waged in math.

Spies send encrypted messages across a shared channel. Hackers listen in and try to crack them. Both sides learn from every win and every loss. Watch their strategies drift in real time.

Cost vs security

Every cipher has a cost (1-10) and a security rating (1-10). Spies pick the cheapest cipher that gets the job done. Would you bolt your diary with a bank vault?

Every time a cipher gets cracked, the spy who used it becomes less likely to use it again. Every time an attack lands, the hacker reaches for it more often.
